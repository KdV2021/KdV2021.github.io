About Me

Welcome to my personal webpage, I am You-Peng Liao, a physics graduate student in National Taiwan University specializing in quantum field theory and computations in scattering amplitude. My research focuses on scattering amplitude method in renormalization group (RG).

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Physics from National Taiwan University and currently serve as a graduate student at National Taiwan University. This site serves as a public repository for my academic work, including open-source lecture materials, personal study summaries, and a curated list of my publications.
